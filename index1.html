About ICCT-SD-2025


The ICCT-SD-2025 is an international conference organized by GNDEC, focusing on advancements in Artificial Intelligence, Sustainable Computing, IoT, and Smart Technologies. This event serves as a platform for researchers, academicians, and industry experts to exchange ideas, present findings, and foster innovation.
